For a long time I kept notes in private files that never saw daylight. Most
of them were quick reactions to projects — things I wished I'd figured out
sooner, small decisions that compounded into large ones, details that only
matter if you care a lot about craft.

This is the home for those notes now. Design, software, and the seams where
they meet. Short when short is enough; long when the idea deserves it.

## What you'll find here

- **Process writing** — how I approach real design problems end-to-end, with
  the messy middle included.
- **Systems** — thinking about components, tokens, motion, and the invisible
  architecture that makes products feel coherent.
- **Craft** — type, spacing, interaction — the thousand small choices that
  separate decent work from memorable work.

No schedule, no content calendar. Just writing, when the writing is ready.

— M.
